Easy Suite Add-ons

EasyFile Add-on Modules

Copy these HTML files into your Easy Suite folder (same place as index.html). Offline-first + LocalStorage.

EasyBill – Recurring Billing
Manage subscriptions and recurring invoices (offline-first).
easy.bill.v1
EasyExpenses – Expense Tracker
Track expenses with VAT splits, categories, and exports (offline-first).
easy.expenses.v1
EasyCashflow – Dashboard
Cashflow overview: receivables, payables, VAT, inventory value (reads local data).
easy.cashflow.v1
EasyVAT – VAT201 Assistant
Summarise VAT Output/Input for a period and export reports.
easy.vat.v1
EasyPOPIA – Compliance Toolkit
Consent log, data processing register, retention schedule (offline-first).
easy.popia.v1
EasyAudit – Audit Trail
Document change log and activity trail (local).
easy.audit.v1
EasyProjects – Project Costing
Project register with budget vs actual, links to jobs & invoices.
easy.projects.v1
EasyApprovals – Workflow
Approvals for quotes/POs/expenses with thresholds and sign-off.
easy.approvals.v1
EasyAssets – Asset Register
Fixed asset register with depreciation and maintenance reminders.
easy.assets.v1
EasyTimesheets – Time Tracking
Timesheets with billable hours and export to invoicing.
easy.timesheets.v1
EasyLeave – Leave Management
Leave balances, requests, approvals, public holidays list.
easy.leave.v1
EasyCredit Control – Debtors
Debtor ageing, reminders, interest calc, statements exports.
easy.credit.v1
EasyBudgets – Budgeting
Budget caps, variance vs spend, alerts.
easy.budgets.v1
EasyPOS – Point of Sale
Simple POS: cart, totals, receipt print, stock adjust (offline).
easy.pos.v1
EasyContracts – Agreements
Contract templates, renewals, signature capture placeholder.
easy.contracts.v1
EasyInsights – Business Intelligence
Local analytics: trends, top customers, risk flags (basic heuristics).
easy.insights.v1
